Our Products

A Cone for Every Occasion

From the classic cake cone to allergen-friendly options, Joy Baking Group offers the widest variety of quality baked cone products on the market.

🍦

Cake Cones

Classic flat-bottom cones for retail ice cream. Light, crispy, and perfect for self-serve and pre-packaged ice cream formats.

🍧

Sugar Cones

Rolled wafer-style cones with a sweet, golden flavor. The iconic choice for premium scooped ice cream in any retail or food service setting.

🧇

Waffle Products & Bowls

Hand-crafted style waffle cones and edible bowls for premium dessert presentations, parfaits, and specialty menu items.

🌾

Gluten-Free Cones

Certified gluten-free cones that meet the growing consumer demand for allergen-friendly ice cream options without sacrificing taste or texture.

🏷️

Private Label

Custom-branded cone products under your own label. Full co-packing and private-label manufacturing capabilities through our Bodean's division.

⚙️

Custom Formulations

Work directly with our R&D team to develop unique cone shapes, sizes, flavors, or formulations tailored to your specific product needs.
